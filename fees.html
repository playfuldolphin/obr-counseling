Fees & Insurance

Session Fees

Individual Therapy (50 minutes) $225
Couples Therapy (50 minutes) $225

The practice offers a complimentary 15-minute consultation to discuss your needs and answer questions about services and costs.

Fees are due at the time of service. Payment accepted via credit card, HSA/FSA card, or bank transfer.

Insurance

Out-of-Network Provider: Olivia Rosenzweig Mental Health Counseling is currently out-of-network with all insurance plans. However, many clients are able to receive partial reimbursement through their out-of-network benefits.

How Out-of-Network Reimbursement Works

  1. You pay the full session fee at the time of service
  2. The practice provides you with a detailed receipt (superbill)
  3. You submit the superbill to your insurance company
  4. Your insurance reimburses you directly based on your out-of-network benefits

Reimbursement rates vary by plan. It's recommended you contact your insurance provider to inquire about your out-of-network mental health benefits before beginning services.

Questions to Ask Your Insurance

  • Do I have out-of-network mental health benefits?
  • What is my out-of-network deductible?
  • What percentage does my plan cover for out-of-network mental health services?
  • Is there a session limit per year?
  • What information do I need to submit for reimbursement?

Payment Methods

  • Credit cards (Visa, Mastercard, American Express, Discover)
  • Debit cards
  • Health Savings Account (HSA) cards
  • Flexible Spending Account (FSA) cards
  • Bank transfer (ACH)

Payments are processed securely through HIPAA-compliant payment systems.

Cancellation Policy

48 Business Hours Notice Required: Sessions must be cancelled or rescheduled at least 48 business hours in advance to avoid a full session fee.

Late Cancellations: Cancellations made with less than 48 business hours' notice will be charged the full session fee.

No-Show Policy: Missed appointments without notification will be charged the full session fee.

Exceptions may be made for emergencies or unforeseen circumstances. Please communicate with the practice as soon as possible if you need to adjust your appointment.

Good Faith Estimate

Under the No Surprises Act, you have the right to receive a Good Faith Estimate explaining how much your medical care will cost. You can request a Good Faith Estimate for any health care service, including psychotherapy.

For questions or to request a Good Faith Estimate, contact the practice at (332) 296-6057.

Sliding Scale & Financial Hardship

A limited number of reduced-fee slots may be available for clients experiencing financial hardship. Please inquire during your initial consultation to discuss options.
