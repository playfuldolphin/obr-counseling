Frequently Asked Questions

Common questions about therapy services, treatment process, and getting started

Getting Started

Begin by submitting a consultation request through our contact form or calling (332) 296-6057. We'll schedule a complimentary 15-minute phone consultation to discuss your needs, answer questions, and determine if Olivia Rosenzweig Mental Health Counseling is a good fit for you.

The initial session focuses on understanding your concerns, history, and treatment goals. We'll discuss confidentiality, informed consent, and answer any questions you have about the therapeutic process. This is also an opportunity for you to assess whether the therapeutic relationship feels right for you.

Treatment duration varies based on individual needs and goals. Some clients benefit from short-term focused work (3-6 months), while others engage in longer-term therapy (1-2 years or more). We'll regularly assess progress and adjust treatment plans as needed.

Current availability changes regularly. Please contact us to inquire about openings for individual therapy, couples therapy, or group therapy. If we're at capacity, we may be able to provide referrals to other qualified clinicians.

Yes. All therapy communications are strictly confidential and protected under HIPAA and New York State law. What you share in therapy stays private and secure. Limited exceptions to confidentiality exist only in situations involving risk of harm to self or others, child or elder abuse, or when disclosure is required by court order. These exceptions are rare and will always be discussed with you. Your privacy and confidentiality are fundamental to the therapeutic process.

Treatment Approach

Research shows that telehealth therapy is as effective as in-person treatment for most concerns, including trauma and compulsive behaviors. Many clients appreciate the privacy, convenience, and accessibility of telehealth. We use secure, HIPAA-compliant video platforms to ensure confidentiality.

Treatment integrates multiple evidence-based approaches including psychodynamic therapy, cognitive-behavioral therapy (CBT), attachment theory, trauma-informed care, and CSAT methodology. For detailed information about our clinical expertise and treatment approach, visit our Clinical Expertise page.

Insurance & Fees

Olivia Rosenzweig Mental Health Counseling is currently out-of-network with all insurance plans. However, many clients receive partial reimbursement through their out-of-network mental health benefits. We provide detailed receipts (superbills) that you can submit to your insurance company. See our Fees & Insurance page for more details.

Individual therapy sessions (50 minutes) are $225. Couples therapy (50 minutes) is $225. Group therapy (90 minutes) is $75 per session. For complete fee information, visit our Fees & Insurance page.

A limited number of reduced-fee slots may be available for clients experiencing financial hardship. Please inquire during your initial consultation to discuss options.

We require 48 business hours' notice for cancellations or rescheduling. Cancellations with less than 48 business hours' notice or missed appointments without notification will be charged the full session fee.
