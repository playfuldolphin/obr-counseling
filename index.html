About Olivia Rosenzweig, LMHC, CSAT

Olivia Rosenzweig is a Licensed Mental Health Counselor (LMHC) and Certified Sex Addiction Therapist (CSAT) specializing in the treatment of compulsive sexual behavior, betrayal trauma, and relational healing.

With extensive training in trauma-informed care and attachment-based approaches, Olivia provides structured, evidence-based psychotherapy for individuals, couples, and groups throughout New York State via secure telehealth.
